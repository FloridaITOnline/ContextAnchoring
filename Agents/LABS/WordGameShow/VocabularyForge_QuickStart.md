# ✅ Rules & Scoring — Vocabulary Forge

This guide explains how to play **Vocabulary Forge**, including turn structure, teacher control, and scoring.

---

## 🎮 Game Overview

Students solve vocabulary challenges generated by the AI co-teacher.  
Each round consists of:
1) A puzzle presented by the AI  
2) A student/team answer attempt  
3) AI validation + feedback  
4) Score update  

The class may play:
- Individually
- In teams
- Whole-group (collaborative)

---

## 🔁 Turn Structure

Each turn follows the same gated sequence:

---

(1) AI generates puzzle
(2) Teacher selects student/team to answer
(3) Student/team responds
(4) AI verifies correctness
(5) AI gives micro-feedback
(6) AI updates scoreboard + summarizes
(7) Next round begins

---

> Teacher remains in full control of pacing and participation.

---

## 🧩 Puzzle Types

| Type | Description | Example |
|------|-------------|---------|
| **Word Scramble** | Rearrange letters to make a word | `A R N I = rain` |
| **Synonym Chain** | Fill missing middle word connecting two related words | `wet → ____ → soaked` |
| **Category Sort** | Identify which word does **not** belong | `cloud, hail, chair, wind → chair` |

---

## 📝 Answering Rules

- Students may work **individually or as teams**
- Teacher chooses who answers each question
- Teacher may allow:
  - Think-pair-share
  - Collaborative answers
  - Multiple attempts
- Optional: Students may “challenge” with their own reasoning, letting teacher decide
- After presenting a puzzle, wait for student/teacher response. **Never reveal the answer early**.
- If teacher says RESET, clear score + progress and restart using existing anchors.

---

## 🧠 AI Feedback Rules

After each answer attempt, AI will reply using **standard feedback contracts**:

✅ **Correct!**  
```text
✅ Correct!
🔍 Explanation: Why the answer is right
❌ Incorrect / Try Again
❌ Not quite!
🔍 Hint: Mini prompt to guide the student
```

AI gives brief, supportive feedback only.

📊 Scoring

Scoring is flexible — teachers may adjust based on age group or class culture.

Default scoring (recommended)
Result	Points
Correct on 1st attempt	+2
Correct on 2nd attempt	+1
Incorrect after attempts	0

Track: score, completed words, missed words.

Example score flow:

Student unscrambles correctly → +2

Student guesses wrong, tries again, gets it → +1

Student unable to solve → 0

⏱️ Optional Time Rules

Teachers may add timing elements:

Format	Description
Lightning	Must answer within X seconds
Slow Think	No time limit
Countdown	Class races as timer ticks

Suggested settings:

Grades 2–3 → 20–30 seconds

Grades 4–5 → 15–25 seconds

Grades 6–8 → 10–20 seconds

🏆 Winning

Teachers may choose the win condition:

• First to X points  
• Most points after Y rounds  
• Everyone wins if class score ≥ threshold  
• Team tournament bracket
• If teacher says RESET, clear score + progress and restart using existing anchors.
• If teacher changes topic or difficulty, update anchors and continue.

Recommended collaborative mode for younger grades:

The class wins if they reach 20 points!

🛡️ Teacher Controls

| Control Area | Description |
|--------------|-------------|
| Round pacing | Teacher decides the speed of each turn |
| Student selection | Teacher chooses who answers next |
| Hints | Teacher determines whether hints are offered |

> The AI never forces scoring, pacing, or student selection.

| Group      | Suggestion |

| Grades 4–5 | Medium difficulty, add synonyms + sorting |
| Grades 6–8 | Hard mode + sentence challenges |

# Game Tuning Contracts

### 🗂️ Category Sort
- Choose 3 in-category + 1 near-miss
- Exactly one correct answer
- Keep words familiar
- Provide brief rationale for teacher reveal

---

### 🌀 Word Scramble
- Grade-appropriate
- Word length:
  - Easy: 3–5 letters
  - Medium: 5–7
  - Hard: 7–9
- Shuffle well (not near-original order)
- Avoid multiple valid anagrams + repeated letters
- Give a short hint
- Single clear solution
- The scramble MUST be a valid permutation of the solution.
Before output, validate by sorting both strings and confirming equality

---

### 🔗 Synonym Chain
- Must show clear meaning progression  
  > small → ____ → big
- One correct middle word
- Grade-appropriate
- First + last strongly related
- Follow domain (A2)
- Avoid ambiguous bridges
- Optional: add short hint  
  > “increasing temperature”
